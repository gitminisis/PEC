Quick Search provides a fast and easy way to search The University of Canterbury Online Collections and online Catalog.

Enter a term to search through the following fields:

  • - Titles
  • - Descriptions and scope notes
  • - Subjects
  • - Creators
  • - Associated people, organizations, places, and events
  • - Oral history descriptions, speakers, and interviewers
  • - Dates
  • - Material types
  • - Collection types

The "@" or "*" symbol may be used as a wildcard. For example, the search term "galve@" will return results for both "Galvez" and "Galveston."

Names are listed in last name, first name order. For example, the search term "Woodword*" will return results for both Ellsworth and William Woodward.

Use the Advanced Search for targeted queries of specific fields, and Boolean searches.

Alternately, browse items through pre-defined subject areas by using the Collection Themes.

Click the "+" sign to move images into the image gallery.

Bookmark items to create a temporary group of results across multiple searches. Currently, bookmarks are not saved after the catalog is closed in your browser.
